Cancellation & Returns Policy

Order Cancellation

You can cancel your order before it has been shipped. Once the order is shipped, cancellations are not allowed. To cancel your order, please contact our customer support team with your order details.

Return Policy

If you receive a defective or incorrect product, you can return it within 7 days of delivery. The product must be in its original condition, with all tags and packaging intact.

Steps to Initiate a Return

1. Contact our customer support team within 7 days of delivery.
2. Provide your order number, details of the product, and the reason for return.
3. Our team will guide you through the return process.

Refund Policy

Once we receive and inspect the returned product, we will process your refund within 7-10 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original method of payment or as store credit, as per your preference.

Exchange Policy

If you would like to exchange a product, please contact our customer support team within 7 days of delivery. The product must be in its original condition, with all tags and packaging intact. Exchanges are subject to product availability.
