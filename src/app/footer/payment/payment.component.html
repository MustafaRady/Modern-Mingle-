Payments in Lebanon

Accepted Payment Method

We currently accept the following payment method for orders within Lebanon:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Pay with cash when your order is delivered to your doorstep.

Payment Instructions

Cash on Delivery (COD)

1. Select 'Cash on Delivery' during the checkout process.

2. Prepare the exact amount in cash to pay upon delivery, as our delivery personnel may not carry change.

Payment Policies

Payment Confirmation: Our delivery personnel will provide you with a receipt upon receiving your payment.

Refund Policy: Refunds are handled in accordance with our Cancellation & Returns Policy. Please contact our support team if you have any issues.

Payment Security: Paying with cash on delivery ensures that you only pay when you receive your order.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I select Cash on Delivery as my payment method?
A: During the checkout process, simply select 'Cash on Delivery' as your payment method.

Q: Do I need to prepare the exact amount?
A: Yes, please prepare the exact amount as our delivery personnel may not have change.

Q: What happens if I am not available to receive my order?
A: If you are not available, our delivery personnel will attempt to contact you to reschedule the delivery. Please ensure that you provide a valid phone number during checkout.
